A Flawless Global Launch for Innovate Inc.

Published on September 13, 2025 • Production & A/V Management

View Gallery

About The Day

When tech giant Innovate Inc. decided to unveil their groundbreaking new product to the world, they needed an event that matched their reputation for excellence and forward-thinking. Xotic Events was entrusted to produce a seamless, high-impact global launch event that would captivate a live audience and a worldwide online viewership. The result was a technologically advanced and flawlessly executed brand experience.

Event Details

  • Client: Innovate Inc.
  • Location: The Grand Tech Auditorium, Bengaluru
  • Theme: "Future Forward: Unveiling Tomorrow"
  • Services: Full-Scale Event Production, Stage Design, A/V & Lighting Management, Live Streaming, Guest & Media Relations

Engineering a World-Class Experience

The core of this event was precision. Our primary goal was to create an immersive environment that highlighted the sophistication of the new product. We designed a state-of-the-art stage with a curved 4K LED video wall that served as a dynamic backdrop for presentations and product reveals. The lighting and audio systems were synchronized with on-screen content to create dramatic, impactful moments throughout the keynote addresses.

  • A multi-camera setup with a dedicated production team ensured a broadcast-quality live stream to over 50,000 virtual attendees.
  • We constructed interactive product demonstration pods where media and VIPs could get hands-on experience.
  • The post-event networking reception featured curated catering and a relaxed, professional atmosphere conducive to business conversations.
  • Seamless coordination between our team, the client's marketing department, and venue staff was key to the event's success.

A product launch is more than just an announcement; it's a statement. We are proud to have helped Innovate Inc. make a powerful statement to the global market. For corporate events that require meticulous planning and technical excellence, trust Xotic Events to bring your vision to life.
